Hi, my name is Kaylee Hinton!

I'm a graphic designer who naturally gravitates toward vector-based, flat illustration work, where simple geometric shapes come together to form complex ideas and recognizable objects. Through my work, I aim to create designs that are thoughtful, balanced, and expressive while also developing professional work that serves its purpose. Ultimately, my goal as a designer is to bring ideas to life in a way that feels both visually engaging and resonants with the viewer that leaves a lasting impact, no matter the project composition.
